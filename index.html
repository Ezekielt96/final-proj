Exercise Log

Description:

Why keep an online workout log? This simple workout log is designed to replace and organize a traditional workout journal all in one place. If an individual is going to spend valuable time at a gym and they have desired fitness goals, keeping a log for your workout is important to track progress week by week and month to month basis in order to keep yourself motivated and accountable. Think of this website as your own online personal trainer. Workouts will become more effective because you will know exactly what you're doing each day at each session such as how much weight you lifted and duration of each workout.
